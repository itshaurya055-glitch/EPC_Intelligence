What are the specific requirements for the seismic anchoring of the PDU racks in Sector 7B based on the latest structural revisions?

10:42 AM
smart_toy
Core Intelligence Analysis Processing

Based on the Structural Revision v4.2 (issued Oct 12) and the Seismic Compliance Report (SCR-2023-009), the requirements for Sector 7B have been updated to reflect Seismic Design Category D.

The anchoring specifications for the PDU racks are as follows:

  • Bolt Specification: 4x Hilti HST3-R M12 expansion anchors per rack base plate.
  • Minimum Embedment: 120mm effective depth into C30/37 reinforced concrete slab.
  • Torque Setting: 60 Nm (+/- 5Nm) verified by third-party inspection.
  • Clearance: Minimum 300mm spacing between anchor centers and existing structural reinforcement bars.
